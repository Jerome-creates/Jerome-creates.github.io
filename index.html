What is your design process?

The process I am using contains 4 Gates. The Gate 0 is the launch gate where we define the objectives of your project in particular the goal you want to achieve, the schedule of your project and the technical constraints or else of your project (programming languages, tools etc...) as well as the range of services required. The result of the Gate 0 is a formal contrat. Next will be Gate 1 where the first mock-up will be introduced in order to discuss in more detail the design as well as the site map. The output of Gate 1 is the validated design and site map. This Gate triggers the detailled programming works that shall be presented during Gate 2 which is the project validation before public viewing. After project launch a post-launch review - Gate 3 - is organized in order to integrate potential updates and improvements. Gate 3 will be considered passed once the agreed updates and improvements are applied to the project.
